Duel Masters: AI vs Human

An epic battle between humans and AI in a game of strategy and skill.

Visit the App

Key Features

Registration and Login Page

Securely register and log in to access the game. This feature ensures that only authorized players can participate, providing a secure and personalized gaming experience.

Choose Your Opponent

Select between playing against an AI opponent or another human. This flexibility allows players to enjoy the game in single-player or multiplayer mode, catering to different preferences and skill levels.

Gameplay and Board

Engage in strategic gameplay on a dynamic board. Move your characters, plan your attacks, and outsmart your opponent to win the game. The board is designed to provide a visually appealing and interactive experience.

About the Project

This project was inspired by our passion for AI and gaming. It was developed as part of our portfolio project for Holberton School.
